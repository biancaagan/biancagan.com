About

Bianca is a maker, tinkerer, and fan of research rabbit holes. She is also an RF (radio frequency) coordinator for events in the broadcast and live sound industry, and once in a while does some comms or A2 work.

She received an MPS in Interactive Telecommunications from New York University in 2023 as well as a BA in Recording Arts from Loyola Marymount University in 2018.

Bianca recently finished a Research Residency (2023 - 2024) at NYU's ITP/IMA department, where she was a PComp (physical computing) resident helping students with project prototyping and troubleshooting circuits.
